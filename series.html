Generation Kill

Docudrama, Drama, War

2008

IMDB

Alexander Skarsgård, James Ransone, Stark Sands

Generation Kill follows Bravo Company, 1st Reconnaissance Battalion, 1st Marine Division, through the first 40 days of the Iraq War. The series is based on the book of the same name by Evan Wright, a journalist who was embedded with the Marines during the invasion. The show explores the experiences of the soldiers as they navigate the complexities of war, including combat, camaraderie, and the challenges of leadership.
